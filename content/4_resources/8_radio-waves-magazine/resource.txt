Title: Radio Waves Magazine Issue 12

----

Description: Latest issue of Radio Waves, the quarterly magazine covering independent radio culture, technology, and community media practices across Europe.

----

Content:

This issue features in-depth articles on the resurgence of shortwave radio, profiles of emerging community stations in Eastern Europe, a technical deep-dive into digital audio workflows, and reflections on 50 years of community radio in Italy. Regular sections include equipment reviews, programming ideas, legal updates, and a directory of new stations. This issue includes a special supplement on radio and climate activism, documenting how community radio stations are addressing environmental issues.
